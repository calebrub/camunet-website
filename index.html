About CAMUNET

A Ugandan engineering company built for dependable mechanical assets.

CAMUNET-SMC Uganda Ltd is a dynamic Ugandan engineering and automotive services company dedicated to providing reliable, modern, and comprehensive mechanical solutions to individuals, corporate organizations, NGOs, and government institutions.

Built on professionalism, integrity, and technical excellence, the company specializes in full-spectrum vehicle maintenance, mechanical repairs, industrial machinery servicing, generator and motor support, electrical works, solar power support, vehicle tracking, valuation and inspection, fleet advisory, procurement, construction material supply, and supply of genuine automotive spare parts.

Our Services

End-to-end automotive, mechanical, generator, electrical, solar, and construction support.

01

Automotive Maintenance & Mechanical Works

Routine servicing, diagnostics, engine repairs, brake systems, suspension, steering, cooling, fuel systems, full restoration, roadside assistance, fabrication, welding, and component fitting.

02

Heavy Equipment & Industrial Machinery

Excavators, graders, bulldozers, compactors, tractors, forklifts, hydraulic diagnostics, overhauls, preventive maintenance, and field support.

03

Generator, Motor & Electrical Systems

Generator servicing, installation, commissioning, rewinding, load testing, motor repair, alternator repair, control system troubleshooting, and emergency standby support for critical facilities.

04

Solar Power & Electrical Works

Solar inverter systems, battery backup installations, wiring, power distribution, preventive maintenance, performance checks, and reliable electrical support for homes and organizations.

05

Spare Parts & Accessories Supply

Genuine and high-quality aftermarket spares, batteries, filters, lubricants, electrical accessories, truck parts, and heavy equipment spares.

06

Vehicle Tracking & Asset Protection

GPS tracking, route monitoring, fuel monitoring, anti-siphon solutions, immobilizers, alarms, driver behavior monitoring, and fleet reporting.

Valuation, Inspection & Advisory

08

Consultancy, Outsourcing & Logistics

Fleet management advisory, vehicle sourcing, driver supervision, technical audits, maintenance planning, logistics coordination, and cost-control consultancy.

09

Procurement, Construction Materials & General Supplies

Procurement support, specialized items, construction materials, hardware supplies, general merchandise operations, and project sourcing for client assignments.

Engineering Works & Field Support

Mobile field response, engineering works, technical audits, maintenance planning, equipment documentation, mobile support solutions, and practical project coordination for mechanical and power assets.
