About CAMUNET

A Ugandan engineering company built for dependable mechanical assets.

CAMUNET-SMC Uganda Ltd is a dynamic Ugandan engineering and automotive services company dedicated to providing reliable, modern, and comprehensive mechanical solutions to individuals, corporate organizations, NGOs, and government institutions.

Built on professionalism, integrity, and technical excellence, the company specializes in full-spectrum vehicle maintenance, mechanical repairs, industrial machinery servicing, generator and motor support, vehicle tracking, valuation and inspection, fleet advisory, and supply of genuine automotive spare parts.

Our Services

End-to-end automotive, mechanical, electrical, and fleet support.

01

Automotive Maintenance & Mechanical Repair

Routine servicing, diagnostics, engine repairs, brake systems, suspension, steering, cooling, fuel systems, full restoration, and roadside assistance.

02

Heavy Equipment & Industrial Machinery

Excavators, graders, bulldozers, compactors, tractors, forklifts, hydraulic diagnostics, overhauls, preventive maintenance, and field support.

03

Generator, Motor & Electrical Systems

Generator servicing, installation, commissioning, rewinding, load testing, motor repair, alternator repair, and electrical troubleshooting.

04

Spare Parts & Accessories Supply

Genuine and high-quality aftermarket spares, batteries, filters, lubricants, electrical accessories, truck parts, and heavy equipment spares.

05

Vehicle Tracking & Asset Protection

GPS tracking, route monitoring, fuel monitoring, anti-siphon solutions, immobilizers, alarms, driver behavior monitoring, and fleet reporting.

06

Valuation, Inspection & Advisory

Pre-purchase inspections, mechanical assessments, valuation, accident assessment, repair costing, fleet health audits, and roadworthiness checks.

07

Consultancy, Outsourcing & Logistics

Fleet management advisory, vehicle sourcing, driver supervision, technical audits, maintenance planning, logistics coordination, and cost-control consultancy.

08

Procurement & Construction Material Supply

Procurement support, specialized items, construction materials, hardware supplies, and general merchandise operations for client projects.
