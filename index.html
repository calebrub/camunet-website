About Us

Built for dependable technical work across Uganda.

Camunet Uganda - SMC Limited is a multidisciplinary engineering company providing professional repair, maintenance, servicing, installation, construction, and technical support services across Uganda. We specialize in automotive engineering, electrical systems, surveillance installations, fuel dispensing systems, civil construction works, transportation services, and industrial engineering solutions.

Repair Vehicle, generator, compressor, and fuel system support.
Install Electrical, solar, surveillance, A.C, and automation systems.
Build Civil construction works for buildings, bridges, and roads.
Field-ready teams Service bay precision with nationwide mobility.
